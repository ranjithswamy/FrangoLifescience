Company Profile

Frango Lifescience Pty Ltd specializes in Animal nutrition and health, a pharmaceutical company that is providing a complete solution to the Global Livestock and Poultry industry based on day today needs and challenging arena of the farming. The Australian based company has possessing marketing network all over world with its own well equipped manufacturing units for feed additives, premixes, antibiotics and enzymes. Products are designed and evaluated under research and development team consist of Nutritionist, Microbiologist and Pathologist. The Company’s vision is to become a customer centric pharmaceutical company in the area of nutrition and unmet animal health needs. Frango Lifescience Pty Ltd has pioneered in the discovery and development of various animal nutritional and therapeutic products which can help to improve the general health and productivity of Livestock and Poultry. The company’s innovative approach has resulted in well formulated and evaluated feed additives including multitoxin adsorbent, Hepatic stimulants, immunomodulators, Vitamin and Mineral supplements, Probiotics, Prebiotics, Enzymes, Antibiotic growth promoters and also various therapeutic agents, food grade Disinfectants and Sterilants. Research and Development involved in the progress of newer molecules and formulation to cater the current requirements of animal health industry The Company's expertise and understanding of animal nutrition and health have enabled it to enter into major egg, meat and milk producing continents.

Products

ALTIZYME

A Thermostable granular phytase of bacterial origin


Composition:
Cloned E.coli phytase gene as recombinant protein with phytase (6- phytase) activity of ≥ 5000FTU per gram
Product Features:
The only phytase resistant to protease inactivation in GI tract Its total activity takes place in acidic pH (Between 3.5 to 5.5) ie. before the feed entering into small intestine for absorption E. coli phytase was found to be very stable in digesta supernatant from the stomach Phytase inclusion 500FTU/ kg of feed releases 0.12 - 0.17 % of Available phosphorous from phytate bound Phosphorous which replaces 6 – 8 kilogram of Di-Calcium Phosphate in finished feed
Dosage and Administration:
100 gm per ton of finished feed or as per the recommendation of qualified Avian Nutritionist
Net Weight:
25Kg HDPE Bags
BEGROW

Granular antibiotic growth promoter


Composition:
Each Kilogram contains feed grade Bacitracin Methylene Di-salicylate granules equivalent to 100 gram of Bacitracin
Product Features:
Bacitracin Methylene Disalicylate is an antibacterial feed additive, derived from fermentation of Bacillus licheniformis It aids in prevention and control of different forms of enteritis in poultry and swine and especially active against Clostridia perfringens, the causative agent of necrotic enteritis Increase the gut wall integrity, helping to ensure the absorption of all nutrients which favours to improve feed efficiency and weight gain Not absorbed from the intestinal tract, thus no withdrawal period needed For increased egg production and improved feed efficiency in laying hens during the 15th to 45 week of production
Dosage and Administration:
Broiler and Replacement Chicken: 250- 500 gm per ton of finished feed as a growth promoter and to increase the feed efficiency or as per the recommendation of qualified Avian Nutritionist Laying Hens: 100- 250 gm per ton of finished feed to increase egg production and to increase the feed efficiency from 15 weeks of age to 45 weeks of age or as per the recommendation of qualified Avian Nutritionist To Control Necrotic Enteritis: 1- 2Kg per ton of finished feed during the outbreak of Necrotic enteritis or based on the severity of the infection for 5- 7 days until subsiding of clinical signs followed
Net Weight:
25Kg HDPE Bags
