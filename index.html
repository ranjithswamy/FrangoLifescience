Company Profile

Frango Lifescience Pty Ltd specializes in Animal nutrition and health, a pharmaceutical company that is providing a complete solution to the Global Livestock and Poultry industry based on day today needs and challenging arena of the farming. The Australian based company has possessing marketing network all over world with its own well equipped manufacturing units for feed additives, premixes, antibiotics and enzymes. Products are designed and evaluated under research and development team consist of Nutritionist, Microbiologist and Pathologist. The Company’s vision is to become a customer centric pharmaceutical company in the area of nutrition and unmet animal health needs. Frango Lifescience Pty Ltd has pioneered in the discovery and development of various animal nutritional and therapeutic products which can help to improve the general health and productivity of Livestock and Poultry. The company’s innovative approach has resulted in well formulated and evaluated feed additives including multitoxin adsorbent, Hepatic stimulants, immunomodulators, Vitamin and Mineral supplements, Probiotics, Prebiotics, Enzymes, Antibiotic growth promoters and also various therapeutic agents, food grade Disinfectants and Sterilants. Research and Development involved in the progress of newer molecules and formulation to cater the current requirements of animal health industry The Company's expertise and understanding of animal nutrition and health have enabled it to enter into major egg, meat and milk producing continents.

Products

ALTIZYME    

A Thermostable coated granular phytase of bacterial origin

Composition:

Cloned E.coli phytase gene as recombinant protein with phytase (6- phytase) activity of ≥ 5000 FTU per gram and is derived from submerged liquid fermentation

Product Features:

  • * Thermostable granules
  • * Only phytase resistant to protease inactivation in GI tract
  • * Total activity takes place in acidic pH (Between 3.5 to 4.5) ie. before the feed entering into small intestine for its absorption
  • * E. coli phytase was found to be very stable in digesta supernatant from the stomach
  • * Phytase inclusion 500FTU/ kg of feed releases 0.12- 0.17 % of Available phosphorous from phytate bound Phosphorous which replaces 6 – 8 kilogram of Di-Calcium Phosphate in finished feed

Dosage and Administration:

100 gm per ton of finished feed or as per the recommendation of qualified Avian Nutritionist

Presentation:

25Kg HDPE Bags ( 5 × 5 Kg)

BEGROW      

Granular antibiotic growth promoter

Composition:

Each Kilogram contains feed grade Bacitracin Methylene Di-salicylate granules equivalent to 100 gram of Bacitracin

Product Features:

  • * Bacitracin Methylene Di-salicylate is an antibacterial feed additive, derived from fermentation of Bacillus licheniformis
  • * It aids in prevention and control of different forms of enteritis in poultry and swine and especially active against Clostridia perfringens, the causative agent of necrotic enteritis
  • * Increase the gut wall integrity, helping to ensure the absorption of all nutrients which favours to improve feed efficiency and weight gain
  • * Not absorbed from the intestinal tract, thus no withdrawal period needed
  • * For increased egg production and improved feed efficiency in laying hens administer during 15th week to 45th week of production

Dosage and Administration:

Broiler and Replacement Chicken:


250- 500 gm per ton of finished feed as a growth promoter and to increase the feed efficiency or as per the recommendation of qualified Avian Nutritionist

Laying Hens:


100- 250 gm per ton of finished feed to increase egg production and to increase the feed efficiency from 15th weeks of age to 45th weeks of age or as per the recommendation of qualified Avian Nutritionist

To Control Necrotic Enteritis:


1- 2 Kg per ton of finished feed during the outbreak of Necrotic enteritis or based on the severity of the infection for 5- 7 days until subsiding of clinical signs followed or as per recommendation of Avian Physician

Net Weight:

25Kg HDPE Bags (5 × 5 Kg)

FRETOX      

Multitoxin binder, Mould inhibitor and Pathogen Binder

FRETOX is a unique combination of completely purified selected HSCAS, Organic adsorbents, Buffered organic acid salts, lipotropic agents and antioxidants. It acts with toxins as hydrophobic binding, hydrogen bonding, electrostatic attraction or repulsion, and coordination bonds mechanism and it forms stable and irreversible complexes with the mycotoxins and excreted by the bird.

Product Features:

  • * Alkaline binder and works well at acidic pH (Crop and Proventriculus) rather than in intestine
  • * Broad spectrum - Adsorption and degradation of all mycotoxins in feed
  • * Block the binding of toxins with intestine mucosa and preventing its absorption
  • * Excellent Mould inhibitor, pathogen binder and bactericidal
  • * No binding or damaging vitamins, minerals or other nutritional component of the feeds
  • * Extensively tested in vitro and in vivo
  • * Increases digestion and absorption of nutrients
  • * Good stability during the extrusion and pelleting process of feeds

Dosage and Administration:

Standard inclusion :

1 kg/ ton. or as per the recommendation of qualified Avian Nutritionist

Recommended dose :

0.5 - 2 kg/ ton. of final feed depending on the extent of the mycotoxin contamination

Packing:

25Kg HDPE Bag

TYLOFRAN100

Tylosin phosphate 10 % Feed premix (Granules)

Efficient Economical Macrolide to combat different microbes

Composition: Tylosin Molecule equivalent to 100 gram per kilogram as Tylosin Phosphate

• Macrolide antibiotic, bacteriostatic, with potential for a time-dependent bactericidal action, particularly with high concentrations, derived from Streptomyces fradiae, acting against mycoplasmas, gram positive and gram-negative organism, as well as some Rickettsia sps., and Chlamydia sps

• Tylofran100 possess an antiadhesive activity against Enterobacteriaceae – E. coli, Salmonella spp., in the gut (hindering their adhesion on the intestinal villi, their penetration through the intestinal mucous membrane and impeding their colonization’s phase as well) leads to better FCR and body weight gain

• Compatible with Ionophore anticoccidials, tetracycline, sulphonamides, spectinomycin and the common dietary components of the feeds

Indications:

• Improves weight gain and feed conversion efficiency as a growth promoter

• Effective against tylosin-sensitive pathogenic microorganisms, Mycoplasmosis, Chronic Respiratory Disease, Necrotic Enteritis, Infectious Synovitis in chickens and turkeys, Spirochetosis (Borreliosis) in chickens and Dysentery in pigs.

Mode of administration

• Orally, well homogenized by mixing the measured quantity of the Tylofran 100 (calculated on the basis of the prescribed dose) with a suitable quantity (20-25 kg) of feed ingredients before incorporation in to final mix for thorough dispersion.

Product Features:

• Helps to protect the monogastric animals from Gram-positive, Gram negative bacteria and mycoplasma

• Excellent growth-promoting agent in monogastric animals

• Improves Feed conversion ratio (FCR), Weight gain, Egg production and Hatchability

Dosage and Administration:

Broiler and Chicken (replacements) : 750 - 1000 grams per ton of feed. Administer in feed to chickens 0 to 7 days of age, follow with subsequent administration in feed for 5 days at interval of 3 to 4 weeks or as per the recommendation of qualified Avian Physician

Breeder and Laying Birds : 200 g to 500 g per ton of feed for 5 days once in a month or as per the recommendation of qualified Avian Physician

Withdrawal Period:

For meat: Avian Species (Poultry) – 5 days after the last administration if dosage of Tylofran100 exceeds 800 gram per ton of feed preparation.

PACKING: 1 Kg and 20 Kg HDPE multi-layer paper bags.
